Atmospheric Pictures

Alex Gartner

The first picture was taken recently on my commute from the train station to school. I really liked how low the clouds were. They blanketed the buildings, almost hiding the very tops' of them.

The second picture is a from a few years ago when I was working at Jimmy John's as a delivery driver. Its a beautiful sunset, my coworkers and I all stopped to marvel at it.

The third picture is much older and I believe it was either taken in the early morning or mid evening.The colors of the sky and the moon were very beautiful, I felt compelled to capture the moment.
