WebDev2 - Biography - Meet the Code Monkey

You don't know me!...but now you do!

My name is Alex Gartner. I am a 23 year old man, from Arizona and living in Chicago, studying game programming at Colombia College Chicago. My personal and professional philosophy is one of accountability, integrity, and honesty. I try to live my life and do my work according to these principals. I played my first video game when I was 6, ever since I have been absolutely smitten with how games had the ability to completely immerse the player in completely fictional universes. I cannot wait to immerse players in my games, to steal countless joyful hours from them; Its my calling and my true passion. The accompanying picture is of me in my cook uniform before a shift, I absolutely despise the service industry but something has gotta pay for these dreams.
